Expert Insights

Nutritionist Advice

Foundational rules from our certified dietitians to ensure you get the most out of your food.

Hydration is Key

Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily. Dehydration directly impacts strength output and metabolic rate.

Nutrient Timing

Consume the majority of your daily carbohydrates around your workout window to optimize glycogen storage.

Whole Foods First

Aim for an 80/20 split. 80% whole, unprocessed foods for micronutrients, and 20% flexible fun foods.

Smart Supplements

Supplements shouldn't replace meals. Stick to basics like Whey Protein, Creatine Monohydrate, and Multivitamins.

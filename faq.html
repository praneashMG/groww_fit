Diet & Macros

Nutrition Plans

When you set up your profile, we ask for your age, weight, height, activity level, and ultimate goal (cut, bulk, or maintain). We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to find your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE) and divide your macros optimally from there.

Absolutely. Our meal planning software allows you to filter out any dietary restrictions, including Vegan, Vegetarian, Pescatarian, Gluten-Free, and Dairy-Free options.

Expert Help

Coaching Sessions

Billing

Payments & Refunds

Yes, there are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time directly from the billing section in your dashboard. You will retain access until the end of your current billing cycle.

We offer a 7-day free trial on our Pro plan so you can test it risk-free. Once a billing cycle has started and a charge has been made, we do not offer refunds for that specific month, but you can cancel to prevent future charges.

Account

Account Help

Click the "Login" button at the top right of the page, then click "Forgot Password". Enter the email associated with your account, and we will send you a secure link to reset it.

Tech Specs

App & Device Integrations

Yes, the GrowwFit app seamlessly integrates with Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, and Garmin to pull in your daily step count, active calories burned, and sleep data directly into your dashboard.

Pricing FAQs

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade to Elite or downgrade to Basic at any time from your account settings. Billing will be prorated automatically.

How does the 7-day free trial work?

When you sign up for Pro, you won't be charged for the first 7 days. You have full access to all features. Cancel before day 8 to avoid charges.

Do I need gym equipment?

No! While we have advanced gym programs, we also offer comprehensive bodyweight and dumbbell-only plans perfect for home workouts.

Is the Elite coaching strictly online?

Yes. The Elite plan pairs you with a certified coach through our app. You will receive customized daily feedback, form checks via video, and direct messaging.
